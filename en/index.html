Privacy Policy

SoloWealth — Guaranteed Privacy - Local Storage Only

Last updated: May 15, 2026

The protection of your privacy is the central pillar of SoloWealth. The application is designed according to a principle of absolute privacy by design (Privacy by Design).

Privacy Commitment: No financial, personal or behavioral data is collected, transmitted or stored on external servers. All information entered remains exclusively stored in the local memory of your device.

1. Absence of data collection

SoloWealth does not require any account creation, registration, or identification. Therefore:

  • We do not collect any nominative or contact data.
  • We have no access to your financial data (salaries, budgets, assets).
  • We do not use any third-party tracking or behavioral analysis tools.

2. Local storage and security

Local Storage

All data related to your calculations and simulations are kept only on your device. They are protected by the standard security mechanisms of your operating system (Android/iOS).

As the publisher has no access to any central database, it is technically impossible for us to consult, share or sell your information.

4. Third-party services

SoloWealth interacts with external services only in the following cases:

Market Data

To update exchange rates, the application queries a specialized API. This request is anonymized and does not contain any personal financial information.

Distribution Platforms

During installation or update, Google services may collect anonymous technical metadata (device model, error reports).

5. Regional and International Compliance

QUEBEC

Law 25

In accordance with the Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector of Quebec, SoloWealth guarantees you total control:

  • Right to deletion: Since the data is local, deleting the application results in the permanent and irreversible deletion of all your data.
  • Transparency: As no data is in the custody of the publisher, your personal information never leaves your private sphere.
EUROPE

GDPR

SoloWealth adheres to the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your rights to deletion and data portability are guaranteed by exclusive local storage: you remain the sole owner of your information.

USA

CCPA / COPPA

We do not sell any personal information. SoloWealth respects the principles of the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (COPPA).
